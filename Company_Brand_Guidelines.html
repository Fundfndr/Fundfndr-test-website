01 — Logo

Mark & Wordmark

The FundFndr logo consists of an FF monogram mark inside a circle and the "FundFndr" wordmark in Inter Semibold. Always use the supplied SVG files — never recreate or rasterize.

Primary — use on white & light backgrounds
Reversed — use on navy & dark backgrounds
Minimum size

Nav/UI: 44px height minimum. Footer/marketing: 48px height minimum. Never render below 36px.

Clear space

Maintain a minimum of 24px of clear space on all sides of the logo at all times.

Don'ts

Do not recolor, stretch, rotate, add drop shadows, place on busy backgrounds, or use the mark without the wordmark.

03 — Typography

Two typefaces: DM Serif Display for all headings (elegance, authority), Inter for all UI and body copy (legibility, precision). Never swap or mix roles.

06 — Voice

Brand Voice

FundFndr speaks like a trusted, informed friend — not a sales deck or a robo-advisor. Precise, calm, confident.

AttributeDoDon't
Confident "The clearest way to compare funds." "We try to help investors maybe find…"
Precise "10,000+ ETFs tracked. Updated daily." "Tons of funds. Super up to date!"
Calm "Invest with clarity." "Stop losing money NOW!"
Empowering "Backed by data, not guesswork." "We'll tell you exactly what to buy."
